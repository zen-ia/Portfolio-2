My Journey into Cybersecurity

Author: Zenia | Date: September 23, 2024

When I first started my journey into cybersecurity, I was a curious software engineering student with a passion for protecting systems and exploring vulnerabilities. I always believed technology is only as powerful as its security measures.

Why Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity fascinated me because it combines problem-solving, coding, and continuous learning. I started small by understanding network protocols and tools like OWASP Amass and Wireshark. These tools allowed me to explore how networks operate and where vulnerabilities can occur.

What I Learned

Through practice, I have developed skills in areas like DNS enumeration, creating VPNs, and ethical hacking exercises. Recently, I set up my own VPN server using Kali Linux as the backend and Windows as the client. It was a challenging yet rewarding experience.

Looking Ahead

As I move forward, I aim to contribute to secure system development. My focus will remain on building systems that are not only functional but resilient against attacks.

To all aspiring cybersecurity enthusiasts: Keep learning, stay curious, and embrace challenges. The field is vast, and every day brings something new to discover!
